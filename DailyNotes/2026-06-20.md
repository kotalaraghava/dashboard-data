### Seasonal Guidance (Varsa)
**Monsoon season active; Vata peaks, Agni weakens — support with warmth and digestive spices.** | Weather: ☔ Bangalore monsoon onset

Varsa (rainy season) requires warm, well-cooked, light foods; moisture increases Vata and decreases Agni (Ashtanga Hridayam Ritucharya). Raw foods prohibited; takra (buttermilk) recommended.

4. Jackfruit — grounding, sweet, nourishing foundation during Varsa
5. Dates — warm, sweet-sour, ignites Agni in moisture-heavy season

**Top 5 Vegetables** *(Varsa Ritucharya — Bangalore availability; all cooked, raw prohibited)*

4. Spinach (cooked) — iron-rich, warm preparation for pregnant wife's strength
5. Drumstick (cooked) — light, digestive, naturally Vata-balancing in wet season
