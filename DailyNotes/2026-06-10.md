### Seasonal Guidance (Varsa — Monsoon)
**Surya in Vrishabha (Vasanta base) but Parjanya [rain] active → Varsa per Deshacharya** | Weather: 🌧️ 26°C · 82% humidity · moderate rain

In Varsa, Vata spikes due to cold, damp environment and irregular wind. Agni weakens. Charaka Samhita (Sutra 3.6): light, warm, well-cooked foods; avoid raw, heavy, cold. Takra (buttermilk whey) restores agni. Oil abhyanga (sesame preferred) protects Vata from penetrating damp.

**Top 5 Fruits** *(Varsa Ritucharya — Bangalore availability, pregnancy-safe, Vata-reducing)*
1. **Pomegranate (Dadima)** — sweet, astringent; reduces Vata, aids iron absorption (pregnancy benefit). Cook seeds lightly or eat fresh.
2. **Dried figs (Anjira)** — warm, sweet; nourishing to bones; gentle laxative; safe in pregnancy.
3. **Mango (Ripe, cooked into chutney)** — sweet, warming; season is waning but ripe stored ones available; digestive; stabilizes pregnancy Pitta.
4. **Grapes (black/green)** — cooling but gentle; mild astringent; light; supports kidney function in warm monsoon.
5. **Papaya (half-ripe, cooked)** — digestive, warming; enzyme aids digestion when agni is weak; avoid raw or unripe (risk in pregnancy).

**Top 5 Vegetables** *(Varsa — Bangalore availability, raw prohibited, warm-cooked only)*
1. **Bottle gourd (Lauki/Petha) soup** — light, cooling without chilling; diuretic; Ayurveda recommends heavily in Varsa. Always cooked with ginger + sesame oil.
2. **Ridge gourd (Tori)** — light, heating; supports digestion; easily cooked. Monsoon staple.
3. **Okra/Bhindi (fried in sesame oil)** — warming, light, reduces Vata. Avoid steaming; fry with spices (turmeric, cumin).
4. **Bitter gourd (Karela) — cooked with sesame oil & jaggery** — strengthens agni; detoxifying but temper bitterness with sweetness in pregnancy.
5. **Leafy greens (Spinach/Fenugreek) — cooked in ghee with cumin, ginger** — iron-rich (pregnancy); warm cooking destroys raw property harmful in Varsa.

*Wife (3 months pregnant, baby due October): Varsa + early 2nd trimester = stable energy emerging. Vata aggravation in monsoon can trigger anxiety or constipation. Warm, oiled, cooked foods prevent miscarriage risk. Takra and warm ghee essential.*
