### Seasonal Guidance (Varsa)

**Monsoon onset; Parjanya (rain) active** | Weather: 🌧️ 26°C · 75% humidity · Early monsoon

Varsa peaks Vata and dries Agni — digestion weakens, virility is challenged. Avoid cold, raw foods. Takra (cultured buttermilk) steadies digestive fire. Cooked grains with ghee and warming spices anchor the body.

**Top 5 Fruits** *(Varsa, Bangalore, wife 3 months pregnant)*
1. Mango (ripe) — sweet, grounding; supports haemoglobin; aids fetal growth
2. Papaya — digestive, light, aids elimination; safe in pregnancy
3. Pomegranate — astringent; strengthens blood; cooling yet grounding
4. Watermelon (limited) — hydrating but cold; take warm, with salt
5. Chikoo — sweet, nourishing; supports maternal weight gain

**Top 5 Vegetables** *(Varsa — cooked, warm. Avoid raw.)*
1. Ridge gourd (served cooked in ghee) — light, cooling, aids digestion in Varsa
2. Bitter gourd (with spices) — detoxifying, aids weak Agni
3. Bottle gourd (cooked with warming spices) — cooling yet grounding
4. Okra (cooked in ghee) — light, slippery, aids Vata dryness
5. Spinach (cooked in ghee, sesame oil) — nourishing; iron for pregnancy

**Wife (3 months, Varsa, Ashtami):** Ashtami darkens; Varsa weakens Agni. Warm soups, ghee-cooked vegetables, small frequent meals. Avoid raw salads entirely. Sesame seeds with warm milk in evening for grounding.
